Community Days

We host regular Community Days where you can come along and get involved in the Kin Hill Community. Depending on the season, activities include tree planting, compost turning, fruit picking, apple juicing, and building natural structures.


Hot drinks and home cooked lunch provided. If you're interested in coming along, email us here.
