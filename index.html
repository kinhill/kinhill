Our Purpose

Kin hill is about kinship and exploring relationship in all itâ€™s dimensions. To ourselves, to each other, to the land and animals and to those yet to come that will call Kin Hill their home. We are addressing the current paradigm that humans are an extractive and separated species. We live into our interdependence with all beings remembering that humans can be a beneficial species. The land sustains and nourishes us, we too can nourish the land.

Community Days

We host regular Community Days where you can come along and get involved in the Kin Hill Community. Activities can include tree planting, composting, fruit picking, apple juicing, building natural structures and much more.


Lunch and hot drinks are provided and sometimes the pizza oven gets fired up! Interested? Email us here.
