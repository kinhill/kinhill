Kin Hill Studio

Nestled within the Cumbrian fells, the studio at Kin Hill is a light-filled sanctuary designed for creativity, movement, and gathering. It is a space built with intention, offering a peaceful atmosphere for facilitators, artists, and those seeking a moment of stillness.

The Design

60sqm of open floor space with natural lime-plaster walls and underfloor heating, creating a warm and grounding environment year-round.

Amenities

Facilitators have access to a small kitchenette for refreshments, composting toilets, and outdoor breakout areas including a fire pit.

Versatility

Whether for a yoga retreat, a seasonal craft workshop, or a collaborative meeting, the space comfortably adapts to your needs.
