About the Workshop

For a lot of people the idea of food fermenting can seem scary, but it’s actually our oldest tool to make food last longer, taste better, and actually be safer!

Join us for a hands-on exploration of the microbial world. A sensory deep-dive into the ancient art of lacto-fermentation and probiotic drinks. Discover the excitement of fermentation as we guide you through the science, the stories, and the physical craft of fermenting.

The Day

Sensory Awakening We begin with an invitation to the senses. Experience the fizz, the pop, and the vibrant colors of active ferments—from bright pink krauts to spicy hot sauces and bubbling kefirs.
From Garden to Studio We’ll step out into our vegetable beds and woods to harvest and forage for fresh, seasonal produce. Back in the studio, you’ll get hands-on: chopping, weighing, and packing your own jars.
Science in Action As we work, we’ll demystify the "invisible" work of microbes. You’ll learn the fascinating biology of Lactobacillus and the symbiotic dance of the SCOBY, understanding exactly how salt, sugar, and time transform simple ingredients into superfoods.
A Living Lunch A shared meal featuring a variety of fermented delights. We’ll discuss the human microbiome—and how these ancient foods support our health and rewire our gut.
Troubleshooting Learn to read your ferments. We’ll cover how to spot a happy culture and what to do if things go off-track. You’ll leave with the confidence to experiment without fear.

What You’ll Take Home

  • Your own hand-crafted vegetable ferments and the foundations of a water kefir.
  • The technical knowledge to ferment safely and creatively at home.
  • Access to our online community forum for ongoing support and troubleshooting.
  • A slower rhythm and the skills to collaborate with the trillions of tiny microbes that make our food come alive.
