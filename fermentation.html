About this Workshop

Join us for a hands-on exploration of the microbial world. In this workshop, we will dive into the ancient practice of fermentation, learning how to transform seasonal produce from the Kin Hill garden into vibrant, probiotic-rich foods.

You will learn the science and soul of:

  • Wild-brined Sauerkraut
  • Seasonal Kimchi variations
  • The basics of Kombucha and Water Kefir
